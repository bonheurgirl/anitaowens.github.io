What are New Yorkers most likely to die of?

Hint: It's not what you think it is!

This is probably not so awesome news for the average NYC resident. The data covers the period of 2007 to 2011.

The number #1 killer of Americans is Heart Disease according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and New Yorkers are no exception. Contrary to popular belief, more people die of health related diseases than homicide each year. In fact, the number of homicides is really a tiny percentage of all deaths. It’s extremely rare.

However, heart disease related deaths have declined in recent years. But it’s quite interesting to see the massive numbers of deaths related to health causes.
